How SID Works
Three steps to download resumes
SID lives in your browser and appears automatically when you're on the Indeed employer dashboard.
1

Open a Job's Candidates

Go to the Indeed employer dashboard, open a job posting, and click on a candidate's name. SID appears in the corner.

2

Click Start

Hit the green Start button. SID processes every candidate automatically — downloading resumes and logging details.

3

Download Your ZIP

When the progress bar turns green, click Download Zip or Download All Today to grab your organized files.
